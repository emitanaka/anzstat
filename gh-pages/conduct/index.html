Code of Conduct

Everyone in the slack channel is expected to behave professionally and appropriately at all times. Professional and appropriate behaviour involves scientific and ethical integrity, respectful conduct towards others regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearances, race, religion (or lack thereof) or technology choices. We do not tolerate harassment, sexual or otherwise, by anyone. Anyone violating these rules may be expelled from the slack channel.

Slack channel members agree to at all times:

  • Be considerate in speech and actions and respect others.
  • Refrain from demeaning, discriminatory, or harassing behavior and speech.

  • Threatening behaviour including bullying, threat of violence, deliberate intimidation, stalking or incitement of violence.
